FAQ

Frequently asked questions (FAQ) or Questions and Answers (Q&A), are listed questions and answers, all supposed to be commonly asked in some context, and pertaining to a particular topic.

1. What kind ok jobs I will find here?

We are posting IT jobs from Europe. Jobs can be of any type: commission, volunteer, part-time, internship, full-time, temporary, apprenticeship, contract, permanent.

2. Can I submit my resume?

Not yet! We are a jobs board not a recruiting site.

3. Can I publish a job on your site?

Yes, you can for a small amount of money. We will implement this feature soon!
